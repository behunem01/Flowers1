Traveling

One of my favorite things in the world is traveling. I love everythings about it. Experiencing new cultures, foods, architecture, and nature. This world is so beautiful, and there is so much to explore. One of my favorite trips I have ever taken was in 2018. My family and I went and road-tripped around Europe for a month. We flew in to Paris and spent a few days there. After, we rented a car and drove to Germany for a couple of days. Next up was Switzerland for a few days. Our last country was Italy, and we were traveling around different parts of Italy for 2 weeks. We made the most incredible memories, for which I am so grateful. Below are some photos from the trip.

My mission

I served a mission in Mesa, Arizona. My call was to the Mesa Temple Visitor's Center. It had not opened yet when I arrived in Mesa due to COVID, but I ended up spending a big portion of my mission there, and proselyting in Mesa. I got to learn Spanish, and learn about Mexican culture. I grew closer to my Savior Jesus Christ, and had so many powerful spiritual experiences. I am so grateful for my time there, and miss it every day.
